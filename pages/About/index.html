What is A Heart's Meal?

A Heart's Meal is a project made by Kevin Cadena under the guidance of Jan Fairbarn.

Taking the form of a series of interviews over a home-cooked meal, the overall mission of the project is to explore the idea of home through the lens of food and the sort of different things it brings back to people about home. Please double click any of the plates to view the interviews!

This project was set in Pacifico, Oswald and Lora.
